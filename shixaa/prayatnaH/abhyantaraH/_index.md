- मुखस्थ-प्रयत्नः।
- Indeed, we open the mouth and place the articulator (tongue/ lip) BEFORE producing the sound.

> The _abhyantara_ _prayatna_ is the mode of articulation preparatory to the utterance of the sound. - vAsu
